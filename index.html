Random Notes of Boston

The making of a home

For five years, this city nourished, chastised, and comforted me (in no particular order, and sometimes all at the same time).
It cradled my curiosity and took it to places and people beyond my imagination (for better and for worse).
Anyway, I'm sad to have to leave it.
So here it is-- an ode to Boston, to ease my little heartbreak.
